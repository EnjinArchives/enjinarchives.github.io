In Memoriam

We would like to honor the following users who have tragically passed on too soon. We are both shocked and saddened to learn of their passing and we offer our deepest sympathies to their families in their grief. We miss them too.

We list these users primarily in the interest of preserving their memory for their friends, and allowing their online friends to know that they have departed. In the interest of protecting their privacy, we are limited in how much information we can provide.
  • Clifford (AKA Chris) (Mineplex) - Memorial

    Chris left this world suddenly on 24 October 2022.

    Chris was a user, a creative, and a dear friend to many. His musical talent and friendship will be remembered. He is dearly missed.

    Please visit his YouTube channel if you would like to see some of his past Minecraft videos and offer a word in remembrance.

  • carnlvaL (Mineplex) - Memorial

    Daniel Saniukovich, AKA carnlvaL, tragically passed suddenly on 25 November 2020.

    He was a student at the Unviersity of South Florida, enrolled in the Honors College. Countless words of condolence have poured in since his passing, and he will be remembered and missed dearly. Daniel was predeceased by his mother two years prior.

    Please enjoy this tribute to Daniel's life.
