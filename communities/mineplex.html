The Rise and Transformation of Mineplex

 

 

 

The History of Mineplex

In late 2011, a server called AusMC was created by Chiss. It started as a Factions/Survival/PvP server for the Australian Minecraft community. 

It had custom plugins coded by Chiss, who later created Dominate/Champions for this server. 

 

In late 2012 AusMC closed, so Chiss joined defek7 in creating a new server called NautilusMC. 

The only game available was Dominate (Champions), and the lobby also had Lava and Ruins parkour. 

 

After a few months, they re-launched it as BetterMC, keeping Dominate but also adding Clans and MineKart. 

 

Around the same time, a server called Mc Castle Siege (by Spu_ and sterling) launched. It featured one game, Castle Siege. It was later renamed Mineplex. 

 

On May 24 2013, it merged with BetterMC and opened to the public, the new server being called Mineplex. It was owned by Chiss, defek7, sterling and Spu.

They decided to pay CaptainSparklez and other YouTubers to make videos advertising the server, which made it explode in popularity. 

 

Peak: 35,000 players in 2015

 

 

Mineplex was the most popular Minecraft server in the world. What brought it down?

Bad decisions + bad management = community destroyed itself = people left = no more donators = no more money = shutdown.jpg 

  1. Bad decisions

In 2015 Dooskee became the "head of trainee management". He changed the criteria of accepting staff, and shortly after, bad staff flooded the Mineplex team. This is the start of Mineplex's problems. 

  1. Bad management
  • Mineplex anticheat was basically non existent, and hackers flooded the server. This caused a huge headache for players, and it became frustrating to play Mineplex.
  • Hypixel's anticheat was much better, in every way. Hypixel also banned accounts that logged in from multiple countries in a short time, thus eliminating McLeaks accounts and other alt services usually used by hackers.
  • Hypixel even went as far as to develop a system which detects non-vanilla clients and instantly bans you if you log in using one.
  1. Toxicity/Drama
  • Mineplex had no control over the walls of forum users, since they were using Enjin. Mineplex owner Chiss was also known for constantly creating huge dramas because of his decisions (banning people for exposing hacking staff, "attacking christianity", locking threads randomly, etc). The staff list was also public which led to many people "attacking"/trolling staff members and even hacking them (see: ChuckNorris, xSpencer, LAPD, etc).
  • Hypixel was designed in a smart way to avoid "toxicity". For example, when you died in a game, if you sent messages in the chat the person who killed you wouldn't see them. Also, words such as EZ are blocked. Furthermore, Hypixel uses Xenforo, which means they have full control over every aspect of their site/forums/profiles. This significantly reduces toxicity. Hypixel staff list is also private.

These three things led to the community destroying itself/leaving, which led to Hypixel overtaking Mineplex and Mineplex shutting down now.
