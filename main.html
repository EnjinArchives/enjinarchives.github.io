Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What is Enjin?
Enjin was an online gaming community forum that is now defunct.
What is the purpose of this archive?
The purpose of this archive is to document past users, communities, and events of the Enjin online gaming community forum.
Can I still access the Enjin forum?
No, the Enjin forum is no longer accessible as it is now defunct.
How can I contribute to the archive?
If you have any information, memories, or content related to the Enjin online gaming community forum, you can contribute by contacting us through the provided contact information.
Is this archive affiliated with Enjin or its creators?
No, this archive is an independent project and is not affiliated with Enjin or its creators.
